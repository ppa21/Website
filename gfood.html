About the App

Green Food Challenge is a challenge that Vancouver took a part in to reduce our city's food-based emissions in half by 2020. People usually overlook emissions released in food production since it is a necessity. Currently, we produce 30% more food-based emissions than we should according to a study and if we are to make sure this doesn't cause a long lasting effect, we have to act on it now.

To bring this under limelight, City of Vancouver decided to give CMPT 276 class of Fall 2018 the task to build an android application to aid in their cruisade to reduce food-based emissions in Vancouver.

My Role

  • Computed user's Carbon Footprint based on their food input.

  • Coded to let users add a pledge of how much food-based CO2e they want to reduce annually.

  • Programmed to let users add meals with photos they wish to check out at restaurants.

  • Stored user's pledge and meals on Firebase to be displayed in the application.

  • Added the ability to let users share their pledge and meals to social media.

  • Collaborated with my team to ensure the demo was ready for the sprint review to showcase to the stakeholders every 2 weeks.

  • Got experience developing applications in Android and store and retrieve user data on from database securely.
